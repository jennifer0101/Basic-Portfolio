About Me

I am currently enrolled in the University of Minnesota's coding bootcamp for full stack web development. After doing a variety of volunteer and paid work, as well as attending college for two semesters while being a stay at home parent, I have decided to re-enter the workforce as a web developer.

I am creative, detail-oriented, and enjoy troubleshooting. I really enjoy the challenging, yet satisfying world of coding.

When not learning code, I like to go for walks by the river, snap photos of things that interest me, create art, and connect with friends.
